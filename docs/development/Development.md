### To contribute to the development of QuantstopTerminal the following packages are required:
- [Go 1.15+](https://go.dev/)
- [NodeJS and NPM](https://nodejs.org/en/)
- [Vue CLI](https://cli.vuejs.org/)
- [task](https://taskfile.dev/#/)
